# Design Notes

- Keep the slides minimal, clean, and text-light.
- Use one strong soundbite per slide.
- Make the presentation originator-led: it should sound like one person showing a discovery carefully.
- Design for one listener and one presenter, not a conference audience.
- Avoid dense tables.
- Avoid explaining all 54 cells.
- Use a few strong examples only.
- Prefer large text, strong spacing, and calm visuals.
- Let the dog examples, door examples, and Galway / Irish pressure test carry the story.
- Treat the A-packet as an instrument, not as a wall of theory.
- Keep the tone exploratory: pressure test, candidate validation pathway, not yet independently validated.
