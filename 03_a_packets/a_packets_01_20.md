# A-Packet — Sentences 1–20

## Sentence 1

**Galway City Council approved the new transport plan yesterday.**

### A1 settled_saying
Galway City Council approved the new transport plan yesterday.

### A2 governing_object
The new transport plan’s approval by Galway City Council.

### A3 settlement_work
The sentence moves the plan from undecided or pending status into approved civic status.

### A4 range_scope
The settlement holds within Galway City Council’s decision authority and is temporally bounded by yesterday.

### A5 governing_shape
An official civic decision lands on a transport plan.

### A6 excluded_pull
Do not make “yesterday” or the council as institution the main settlement; the governing event is the approval of the plan.
