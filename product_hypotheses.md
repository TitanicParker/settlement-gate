# Product Hypotheses

These are hypotheses only. They are not proven claims.

## Candidate hypotheses

- Important sentences can be given structural audit receipts.
- Translation systems can be checked for address preservation.
- AI agents can be prevented from acting on unsupported settlements.
- Legal and policy language can be inspected for hidden commitments.
- Sentence-level receipts may become a commercial audit layer.

## Current status

The product direction is exploratory. It should be validated with technical reviewers, translation experts, and potential buyers before any commercial claim is made.
