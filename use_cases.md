# Use Cases

## AI output audit

Check whether an AI-generated sentence has settled more than the evidence supports.

## Translation validation

Compare source and translation addresses to see whether a faithful settlement has been preserved.

## Legal clause review

Inspect legal sentences for hidden commitments, conditions, and blocked rival readings.

## Policy language review

Review public or organizational policy sentences to clarify what they actually settle.

## Agent instruction safety

Prevent agents from acting on sentences that are conditional, unsupported, or over-settled.

## Customer support language

Audit messages such as approvals, denials, refunds, and eligibility statements before they reach customers.

## Public-sector communication

Help ensure public notices, service updates, and civic statements do not overclaim or under-specify commitments.

## Education / writing diagnostics

Teach writers to see what a sentence has settled, what remains unresolved, and where revision is needed.
