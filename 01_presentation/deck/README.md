## Intended use

Anthony can open the deck on his device while the presenter reads from the speaker notes.

The deck is designed for a 30-minute conversation, not a technical lecture. The purpose is to help Anthony appreciate five things:

1. This is not just a language theory.
2. The phenomenon is real enough to investigate.
3. The A-packet is a tool for protection and validation.
4. The commercial pathway could be significant.
5. The next step should be careful protection and trusted review, not broad exposure.

## Simplest takeaway

> This may be early, but it is not vague. It is a testable sentence-audit idea with possible global AI and translation applications, and it needs careful protection before wider exposure.

## Presentation posture

Do not overclaim proof. Present Settlement Gate as an early, testable, potentially practical discovery that needs the right first route through protection, validation, and commercial development.
