# Pitch Notes

<p><strong>Purpose:</strong> keep the Anthony / PorterShed conversation focused, clear, and safe.</p>

<details open>
<summary><strong>One-line positioning</strong></summary>

Settlement Gate is an early-stage sentence-auditing idea: completed sentences settle something, and important settlements should be inspected before they govern action.

</details>

---

## Opening frame

Do not open as if presenting a finished theory.

Open as the originator bringing an unusual but testable discovery for safe guidance.

**Suggested opening:**

> Anthony, I am not asking you to validate the whole theory today. I am asking you to help me take the next step safely.

---

## The simplest explanation

Every completed sentence settles something.

Some settlements are harmless.

Some settlements govern action.

Settlement Gate asks whether a sentence’s landing is earned before that sentence is allowed to govern action.

---

## What to make obvious

The listener should quickly feel that:

- “Did you put the dog?” fails to close.
- “Did you put the dog out, milk?” goes past closure.
- “The door closed” and “The door is closed” are not the same.
- “Visitors may board after ticket inspection” is not unconditional permission.
- Faithful translations should preserve the structural landing.

These examples do more work than technical explanation.

---

## What to avoid

Avoid saying:

- “This proves all sentences have addresses.”
- “This solves translation.”
- “This solves AI safety.”
- “The 54 cells explain everything.”
- “The system is finished.”

Prefer saying:

- “This is a working claim.”
- “This is a pressure test.”
- “This is the phenomenon I want to investigate.”
- “The value is that it becomes testable.”
- “I need help protecting and validating it safely.”

---

## Anthony / PorterShed ask

Ask for help with three routes:

```text
Protect → Validate → Build
```

### Protect

Who should advise on IP, disclosure, and defensibility before wider sharing?

### Validate

Who can test the A-packet, translation claim, and AI-audit claim without overexposing the idea?

### Build

Who can help identify the smallest commercially viable sentence-auditing tool?

---

## Useful introductions

- IP advisor.
- Trusted AI / NLP reviewer.
- Translation or bilingual validation expert.
- Product builder.
- Commercialisation mentor.
- University contact.
- Early-stage funder or innovation advisor.

---

## The sentence to keep returning to

> What did this sentence settle, and was it entitled to settle it?

This is the heart of the pitch.

---

## Closing ask

> Anthony, I am not asking you to validate the whole theory today. I am asking you to help me take the next step safely: protect the idea, identify the right first reviewers, pressure-test the translation and AI-audit claims, and explore whether Settlement Gate can become a commercially viable sentence-auditing tool without losing the discovery status or commercial value.

---

## Desired outcome from the meeting

The meeting succeeds if Anthony can help identify:

1. the first IP conversation;
2. the first trusted technical reviewer;
3. the first translation validation reviewer;
4. the first product/commercialisation pathway;
5. what should not be disclosed yet.
